## Working principles

- Domain computes.
- Builders compose.
- UI presents.
- Formatters control presentation.
- One focused commit per change.

## Foundation

Establish the domain model, investment policy concepts, portfolio facts, rules and deterministic analysis pipeline.

## Core

Turn the domain foundation into stable application flows that help long-term investors understand their portfolio state and decisions.

## Analysis

Build the analysis experience around health, diagnosis, recommendations, impact and summaries that can be consumed by dashboards and assistants.

## Intelligence

Introduce higher-level interpretation, prioritization and future AI-assisted workflows on top of domain outputs without moving business logic into the UI.

## Automation

Support repeatable workflows, scheduled evaluations and assistant-driven actions while keeping persistence and integrations outside the domain layer.

## Future

Expand toward richer scenario comparison, planning workflows and long-term decision support as the domain model matures.
